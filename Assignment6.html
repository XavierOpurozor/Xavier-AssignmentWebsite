How to Become a Cybersecurity Professional

To become a skilled cybersecurity professional, you need both theoretical and hands-on experience. Here's a step-by-step roadmap:

  • 1. Learn the Basics: Understand networking, operating systems (especially Linux), and security fundamentals.
  • 2. Study Cyber Concepts: Dive into malware, social engineering, cryptography, and risk management.
  • 3. Build Technical Skills: Practice with tools like Wireshark, Nmap, Burp Suite, and Splunk.
  • 4. Get Certified: Start with CompTIA Security+, then pursue CEH, CySA+, or CISSP.
  • 5. Join Communities: Follow cybersecurity blogs, join forums like Reddit r/netsec or Discord groups.
  • 6. Practice in Labs: Use platforms like TryHackMe, Hack The Box, and CTF challenges.
  • 7. Build a Portfolio: Document your skills and projects just like this website!
